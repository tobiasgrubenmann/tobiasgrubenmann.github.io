Timebank Ontology: Description back to ToC

In the following, we provide some examples on how to use our timebank ontology.

First, we will create a new user, Alice. We will reuse the FOAF vocabulary the indicate the name of the user. The user's address is expressed using the VCard ontology. 


ex:Alice a tb:User ;
    foaf:familyName "Owl" ;
    foaf:firstName "Alice" ;
    tb:hasAddress ex:VCardA .
ex:VCardA a v:VCard .

Next, we link the skill tb:ComputerRepair and the limitation tb:PoorHearing with Alice. 


ex:Alice tb:hasSkill [a tb:ComputerRepair ;
    tb:hasSkillLevel tb:LevelHigh ;
    rdfs:comment "30 years experience with computers." ] .
ex:Alice tb:hasLimitation [a tb:PoorHearing ;
    tb:hasSeverity tb:LevelLow ;
    rdfs:comment "Preferrably, speak into my left ear." ] .

Alice is available every Saturday and Sunday from 1pm until 6pm, starting from 2019-03-30. We use the Time vocabulary to express this. 


ex:Alice tb:isAvailableOn [
    a time:ProperInterval ;
    time:hasBeginning [time:inXSDDateTimeStamp
        "2019-03-30T13:00:00+08:00"^^xsd:dateTimeStamp ] ;
    time:hasEnd [time:inXSDDateTimeStamp
        "2019-03-30T18:00:00+08:00"^^xsd:dateTimeStamp ] ;
    tb:repeatsOn time:Saturday ;
    tb:repeatsOn time:Sunday ] .
Bob wants to create a new request to ask for help to fix his computer. As Bob owns a dog, he excludes users who have a fear of dogs from the request. Bob offers two time windows where he would be available to receive the help. 

ex:Request a tb:Request ;
    rdfs:comment "My email client fails to synchronize." ;
    tb:requiresSkill tb:ComputerRepair ;
    tb:excludesUserByLimitation tb:FearOfDogs ;
    tb:hasOriginator ex:Bob ;
    tb:hasTimeWindow [
        a time:ProperInterval ;
        time:hasBeginning [time:inXSDDateTimeStamp
            "2019-04-06T14:00:00+08:00"^^xsd:dateTimeStamp ] ;
        time:hasEnd [time:inXSDDateTimeStamp
            "2019-04-06T16:00:00+08:00"^^xsd:dateTimeStamp ] ] ;
    tb:hasTimeWindow [
        a time:ProperInterval ;
        time:hasBeginning [time:inXSDDateTimeStamp
            "2019-04-07T10:00:00+08:00"^^xsd:dateTimeStamp ] ;
        time:hasEnd [time:inXSDDateTimeStamp
            "2019-04-07T12:00:00+08:00"^^xsd:dateTimeStamp ] ] .
Since Alice is available, qualified to help, and not afraid of dogs, she accepts the request. 

ex:Request tb:isAcceptedBy ex:Alice .
Alice spent 1 hour and 30 minutes fixing Bob's computer. This is the accredited duration of the request. Neither Alice nor Bob accumulated any time in the Timebank before, which means that Alice has now a positive time-balance of 1 hour and 30 minutes and Bob has a negative time-balance of 1 hour and 30 minutes (note the negative sign for both hours and minutes of the duration). 

ex:Request tb:hasAccreditedDuration [
    a time:DurationDescription ;
    time:hours 1 ;
    time:minutes 30 ] .
ex:Alice tb:hasTimeBalance [
    a time:DurationDescription ;
    time:hours 1 ;
    time:minutes 30 ] .
ex:Bob tb:hasTimeBalance [
    a time:DurationDescription ;
    time:hours -1 ;
    time:minutes -30 ] .
